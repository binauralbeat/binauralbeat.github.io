ABOUT ME

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT & GRAPHIC DESIGN

I love to create, plain and simple. Throughout my life I've engaged in quite a few creative endeavors from visual arts to music and film editing. Over fifteen years ago I started using Adobe products to create art and promotional material for my music projects. Since then I have formated books created wireframes and a variety of other graphic oriented tasks. Around five years ago I got into HTML & CSS. I always knew that I'd need to learn Javascript or something with more programming power. That's when I decided to enroll at Nashville Software School. While attending classes there I learned not only Javascript, Python and a couple of frameworks but how to be a part of a team and utilize SCRUM methodology.
